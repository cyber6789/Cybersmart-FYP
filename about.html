About Us

Cybersmart is your special place to learn about staying safe while using computers and the internet! We guide you through fun games and activities that teach you how to protect yourself online. Just like wearing a helmet when riding a bike, we'll show you how to use safety tools when you're surfing the web or playing online games. We created this website because we want every kid to become a Cyber Hero who knows how to spot internet dangers and keep their personal information safe. With colourful games, quizzes, and exciting challenges, you'll learn important safety skills while having lots of fun!
