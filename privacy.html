Privacy & Policy

At CyberSmart, your privacy matters to us. We only collect the essential information needed to improve your learning experience and ensure the platform works as intended. We do not collect personal data from children, and no information is stored or shared with third parties. To protect user safety, all communications (like form submissions) are handled securely using trusted methods. We do not require account registration, so there is no username, email, or password stored on our system. CyberSmart is designed to comply with child protection standards including GDPR and COPPA, ensuring that young learners remain safe online while exploring cybersecurity topics. By continuing to use this platform, you accept our approach to safe and anonymous learning. If you would like to know more, please read our full Privacy Policy document.
