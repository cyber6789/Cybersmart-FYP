What is Cyber Safety?

Cyber Safety is all about staying safe while using the internet. It means understanding online risks and knowing how to protect your personal information and well-being. By learning safe online habits, you can explore the internet confidently without putting yourself at risk. Whether you're using a phone, tablet, or computer, practicing cyber safety helps keep your digital experience secure and fun.
